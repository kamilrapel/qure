About QURE

We built the
protein drink
we wanted
to drink.

QURE started with a simple frustration. Every high-protein drink on the shelf was either a sugar bomb, a chalky whey shake, or a chemistry experiment we couldn't read the label of.

We wanted something different: a smoothie that actually delivered serious protein, from a 100% natural source, without any of the usual baggage. No whey. No added sugar. No preservatives. No lactose. Just real ingredients doing real work.

It took us two years of formulation, tasting, and stubbornly saying "no, again" before we had something we'd actually drink ourselves. That's QURE.

What we stand for.

01

Real ingredients

If you can't read it, we won't put it in the bottle. Every ingredient earns its place - or it's out.

02

No shortcuts

No whey, no soy isolates, no maltodextrin, no acesulfame K. Just clean protein and clean flavor.

03

Honest labels

What it says on the bottle is what's in the bottle. No "natural flavors" hiding behind small print.

Built in Poland. Made for the world.

QURE is designed and produced in Poland, in a facility we visit weekly. We work with local suppliers wherever we can, and we hold every batch to the same standard: the one we'd hand to our own family.

Where we're going.

We're a small team with big ambitions. Right now we're focused on getting the core lineup right and into the hands of people who care about what's in their drinks. New flavors, new formats, and new markets are on the way.
